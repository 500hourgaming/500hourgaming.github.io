Some Games I Play

Ark: Survival of the Fittest
Try to survive against other players, constrained within a virtual ring. If you go outside the ring for more than 30 seconds, you instantly die.
Ark: Survival Evolved
A survival game, where you survive on an island with Dinosaurs. You can play with you friends and team up to summon The Brood Mother and the Dragon to get TEK Tier.
ASTRONEER
A Survival game, but in space! You first start out on a planet called Terran. You can build rovers trucks and space ships to fly to other planets.
Geometry Dash
A Rage Game. You have levels to complete, and if you die, you have to restart all over.
Out Of Reach
A survival game with animals. It seems to be mostly about burning houses and raiding, but I think of it as a survival game.
Planet Coaster
A Sand box game if you choose or a challenge. You can build your own theme park with no money in sand box mode, but in challenge mode you have money to buy stuff.
Stranded Deep
A game where you are on a plane and it crashes somewhere in the Pacific Ocean. You take a life boat to the nearest island. It is a very small island, so you have to save materials.
Who's Your Daddy
A game you play with your friends. One person is the baby. The other person is the Dad. The baby is trying to kill himself. The Dad has to stop the baby from killing himself.
Minecraft
A cave game that is a sand box style game with blocks. Your imagination is endless. You can build any thing you want.
